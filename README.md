Drustii AI
Protecting Eyes, Posture & Focus — One Desktop at a Time

Drustii AI is a privacy-first AI wellness and productivity ecosystem designed for students, developers, remote workers, and professionals who spend long hours in front of digital screens.

The platform combines:

Real-time computer vision
Productivity tracking
Wellness monitoring
Focus management
AI-powered assistance

to create a healthier and smarter digital workspace experience.

Problem Statement

Today, millions of people spend more than 8–12 hours daily in front of laptops and screens.

This leads to:

Eye strain
Poor posture
Headaches
Mental fatigue
Loss of concentration
Unhealthy digital habits

Most productivity applications focus only on increasing efficiency but completely ignore user health and wellness.

Drustii AI aims to solve this problem.

Solution

Drustii AI uses real-time computer vision and wellness analytics to help users maintain healthy digital habits while improving productivity.

The system continuously monitors:

Screen distance
Posture
Blink rate
Focus sessions
Productivity patterns

and provides real-time alerts and wellness guidance.
